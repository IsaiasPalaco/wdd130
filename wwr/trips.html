Victoria Falls

Enhance your rafting adventure with our thrilling 2-3 day experience. The Zambezi River, flowing below Victoria Falls, is renowned as the worlds premier rafting destination. Its deep, channeled waters feature class 3 to 5 rapids interspersed with serene pools.

Moatize Adventure

Embark on a thrilling adventure in the Moatize river. Hike through lush meadows, explore charming villages, and breathe in the fresh mountain air.

Cultural Immersion in Tete

Immerse yourself in the rich history and traditions of Tete, Mozambique. Visit cultural vilages, stroll through Imbondeiro groves, and witness a meat festival.
